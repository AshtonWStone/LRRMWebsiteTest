Memorial Bricks

Support the Little River Railroad and Lumber Co. Museum while creating a lasting tribute to the people and moments that matter most. By purchasing a custom engraved brick, you become a permanent part of our museum grounds. Each brick can be personalized with a name, a special date, a dedication, or a meaningful message. In addition to your customized brick, you also receive a complementary 1-year membership to the museum. This membership goes into effect after the purchase and gives the holder a 20% discount on all future purchases at our gift shop and early access to any new products during that year.
